# Privacy Policy

Audio Cutter processes your audio on your device to trim and export clips. We do not upload your audio files to our servers.

## Data we access
- Audio files you choose to import for editing.
- Storage permissions required to import and export clips.

## Third-party services
Audio Cutter uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for advertising and analytics. Review Google's privacy policy for details.

## Your choices
- You can revoke storage permissions at any time in Android settings.
- You can reset your ad ID in Android settings.

## Contact
If you have questions about this policy, contact support at support@workwavestudio.com.
