# Audio Cutter - Store Listing Draft

## Short description
Trim and export audio clips with preview, speed control, and multiple formats.

## Full description
Audio Cutter is a fast, on-device audio trimmer built for ringtones, notifications, podcasts, and loops. Import any audio file, preview a selection, and export a clean clip in seconds.

Key features:
- Import audio from your device and preview before trimming.
- Precise trim controls with waveform feedback.
- Export in MP3, AAC/M4A, OGG, FLAC, or WAV.
- Choose quality presets for size or fidelity.
- Adjust playback/conversion speed from 0.1x to 4x.
- Light/dark/system theme toggle and onboarding walkthrough.
- Processing happens on your device for quick results.

Whether you are making a ringtone or cutting a section from a long recording, Audio Cutter keeps the flow simple and reliable.
